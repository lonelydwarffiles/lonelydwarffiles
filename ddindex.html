Nevernight

Jay Kristoff



"People often shit themselves when they die.

Their muscles slack and their souls flutter free and everything else just... slips out."

—Jay Kristoff's Nevernight



"Arterial spray hissing through his fingers.

Realization dawning in his eyes—shes's killed me, O, God, she's killed me—turning around in a fury, and he whirled on the girl, hands outstretched, fingers curled into claws."

—Jay Kristoff's Nevernight



Review:

My thoughts on Nevernight; it's damn amazing, beautifully written. Crafted by an elegant hand sweeping over the paper with a pen. The thoughts and desires of the author's imagination shaping the ink into words that convey a style of writing I've never seen before. It's a book that I'll come back to many times, will read any and all in its series, and recommend to everyone. So... All in all, it was a fantastical experience to read.
